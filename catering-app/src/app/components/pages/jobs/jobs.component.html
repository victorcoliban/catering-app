DO YOU HAVE WHAT IT TAKES?

Garbanzo is adding three part-time positions.

Do you consider yourself a people-loving, fast-learning, energetic, detail-oriented, all-around team player who needs a new job?

Are you able to stand and move for a 5-hour shift even when it’s 40+ degrees while keeping a cool head and a positive attitude? Can you bend and lift up to 20 kg.? Are you available to work Saturdays?

Restaurant experience is preferred, but if you have the above qualifications, we are willing to train you from the ground up! You will be trained to work in all the areas of our street food market stand: customer service, dish washing and cleaning, opening and closing, food production, barista and line cook.

At Garbanzo, we believe many businesses’ bad behaviour disproportionately hurt most marginalised people in our society, including BIPOC and LGBTQ+ people. Hence, we strive to provide an inclusive, safe and supportive workspace for people with these identities and encourage them to apply with us.

Qualifications:
